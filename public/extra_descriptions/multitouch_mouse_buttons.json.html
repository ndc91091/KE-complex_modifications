Multitouch Mouse Buttons changes 1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9 keys to mouse buttons while your finger is on the trackpad.

Key Mouse button
1 left button
2 right button
3 middle button
4 button4
5 button5
6 button6
7 button7
8 button8
9 button9

Multitouch Mouse Buttons requires MultitouchExtension. Please follow this instruction before using Multitouch Mouse Buttons.

  1. Open MultitouchExtension app from Preferences > Misc.
  2. Enable "Start at login" on MultitouchExtension Preferences.
